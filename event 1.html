Event Name:
ICT Maghreb 2025 – International Information and Communication Technology Exhibition

Dates:
April 21 - 23, 2025

Venue:
SAFEX - Palais des Expositions, Pavilion A, Algiers, Algeria

What is ICT Maghreb?

ICT Maghreb is one of Algeria’s most important ICT events. It brings together professionals, tech innovators, startups, and decision-makers to showcase and explore the latest in digital technologies, cybersecurity, AI, cloud computing, and more.

What to Expect?

  • 200+ Exhibitors from Algeria and abroad
  • 100+ Startups showcasing new innovations
  • Conferences & keynote talks with international experts
  • New tech trends in cybersecurity, cloud, AI, and emerging tech
  • Networking opportunities with professionals, business leaders & developers

Who Should Attend?

  • IT professionals & engineers
  • Startup founders & tech innovators
  • Government officials & decision-makers
  • Students passionate about digital transformation

How to Attend?

Entry is usually free, but registration is recommended. Check the official website to register and for event updates.
